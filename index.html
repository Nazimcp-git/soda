Frequently Asked Questions

What is your return policy?

+

We offer a 30-day return policy on all our products. If you're not completely satisfied with your purchase, you can return it for a full refund or exchange. Custom-made orders are final sale.

How long does shipping take?

+

Standard shipping typically takes 7-14 business days. For larger items, we use a specialized delivery service which may take up to 3-4 weeks. You will receive a tracking number as soon as your order is dispatched.

Do you offer fabric swatches?

+

Yes, we do! You can order up to five free fabric swatches directly from our website to see and feel the materials in your own home before making a decision.
